断面積とマッハ数の関係式は
$$\frac{\text{M1}}{\text{M2} \left(\frac{(\kappa -1) \text{M1}^2+2}{(\kappa -1) \text{M2}^2+2}\right)^{\frac{\kappa +1}{2 (\kappa -1)}}}$$
$$ \left(\frac{\beta M^2}{\omega M^2+2}\right)^{\gamma } \frac{\beta }{2 \gamma M^2-\omega }  $$
ただし　$ \beta =\gamma + 1 $ ， $ \omega =\gamma -1 $
